Bangladesh is full of notable places to explore, many with significant historical value to the region and the world. Some of the most popular landmarks in Bangladesh are Laboni Beach, Shrine of Hazrat Shah Jalal, Dhaka Zoo, and Inani Beach, among many more.

Flying into Cox’s Bāzār Airport is typically the best way to visit Laboni Beach, since it is the closest airport to the area.
